About the Product

LIMITED EDITION

ONLY AT SEPHORA

A set of two mini-size Bright Crystal Absolu Eau de Parfum and Bright Crystal Eau de Toilette fragrances.

Fragrance Description:

Bright Crystal Absolu Eau de Parfum is a fragrance made even more intoxicating by its olfactory concentrations and prolonged persistence, this scent is for a woman who entrusts her femininity. Bright Crystal Eau de Toilette is inspired by a mixture of Donatella Versace's favorite floral fragrances, Bright Crystal is a fresh, sensual blend of refreshing chilled yuzu and pomegranate mingled with soothing blossoms of peony, magnolia, and lotus flower and warmed with notes of musk and amber.

This set contains

- 0.16 oz/ 5mL Bright Absolu Eau de Parfum

- 0.16 oz/ 5 mL Bright Crystal Eau de Toilette

Ingredients

Bright Crystal Absolu Eau de Parfum:

Alcohol, Denat. (Sd Alcohol 39-C), Fragrance, Water, Butylphenyl, Methylpropional, Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate, Hydroxysohexyl 3-Cyclohexene Carboxaldehyde, Linalool, Citronellol, Ethylhexyl Salicylate, Butyl, Methoxydibenzoylmethane, Limonene.

Bright Crystal Eau de Toilette:

Alcohol, Denat. (Sd Alcohol 39-C), Fragrance, Water, Butylphenyl, Methylpropional, Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate, Hydroxysohexyl 3-Cyclohexene Carboxaldehyde, Linalool, Citronellol, Ethylhexyl Salicylate, Butyl, Methoxydibenzoylmethane, Limonene, Geraniol, Ci 17200 (Red 33), Ci 15985 (Yellow 6).

How to Use

Suggested Usage:

-Fragrance is intensified by the warmth of your own body. Apply in the creases of your knees and elbows for a longer-lasting, stronger scent.

-After applying, avoid rubbing or dabbing skin. This breaks down the fragrance, causing it to wear off more quickly.

-If you prefer placing fragrance on your wrists, be sure to reapply after frequent hand-washing, as this tends to rinse off the scent.

-Replace fragrance after 12 months. Expired perfumes more than a year old lose the integrity of the original scent.

Eau de Toilette or Eau de Parfum?:

The difference lies in the volume of perfume oil. While EDT contains five to nine percent, EDP contains more, usually eight to 14 percent. EDPs, therefore, last longer and smell more intense.
